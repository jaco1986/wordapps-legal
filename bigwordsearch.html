Privacy Policy — Big Word Search

Last updated: 28 June 2026

Big Word Search ("the app", "we", "us") is published by BTH. This policy explains how the app handles your data. In short: the app works offline and needs no account. It uses a small amount of anonymous usage analytics to help us improve, and the only other information that leaves your device relates to optional in-app purchases handled by our payment providers. None of this identifies you personally.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, or location.
  • The app contains no ads and uses no advertising networks or advertising identifiers.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • No account, sign-in, or registration is required to use the app.

Information stored only on your device

Your puzzle progress and found words, daily streak, statistics, hints, and settings are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us, and is permanently removed when you uninstall the app.

Anonymous usage analytics

We use PostHog (hosted in the EU) to understand how the app is used so we can improve it. We attach a random identifier generated on your device that is not derived from any device or personal identifier and does not identify you. We record product events such as starting and completing a puzzle (its difficulty and theme, how long it took, and how many hints were used), continuing a daily streak, viewing the upgrade screen, completing a purchase, and whether you enabled or tapped the daily reminder, together with coarse, non-identifying device and diagnostic information. We never send the puzzle grid or the words themselves, any text you enter, or any personal or advertising identifiers. See PostHog's Privacy Policy.

Purchases

If you buy a subscription or the lifetime upgrade, the payment is processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play, and entitlements are managed through our purchases provider, RevenueCat, Inc. To enable and restore purchases, RevenueCat processes:

  • an anonymous app-user identifier generated on your device;
  • purchase and transaction details and your subscription status;
  • basic device and platform information needed to validate the purchase.

This information is used solely to deliver and restore your purchases and premium access. It is never used for advertising. RevenueCat acts as our data processor — see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy. Your actual payment details (card, billing information) are handled entirely by Apple or Google; we never see or store them.

Notifications

The app can show an optional local daily reminder. These notifications are scheduled on your device only. No notification server is used and no data is sent to us.

Children

The app is intended for a general, adult audience and is not directed at children under 13.

Your rights

Because your puzzle data is stored only on your device, you can erase it at any time by deleting the app. Our analytics use only an anonymous identifier and are not tied to your identity; reinstalling the app generates a new one. For purchase-related data held by RevenueCat, you may request access or deletion by contacting us. Depending on where you live (for example, the EEA/UK under the GDPR, or California under the CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above will reflect any changes.
