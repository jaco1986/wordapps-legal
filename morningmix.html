Privacy Policy — Morning Mix: Daily Brain Games

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Morning Mix: Daily Brain Games ("the app", "we", "us") is published by BTH. This policy explains how the app handles your data. In short: the app is built to be private by design — it works offline, needs no account, and shows no ads. The only information that leaves your device is anonymous usage analytics and the data needed to process optional in-app purchases.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, or location.
  • No account, sign-in, or registration is required to use the app.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites, and we do not use the advertising identifier for tracking. The app contains no advertising.

Information stored only on your device

Your daily games, progress, daily streak, badges, statistics, and settings are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us, and is permanently removed when you uninstall the app.

Anonymous usage analytics

We use PostHog (hosted in the EU) to understand how the app is used so we can improve it. We attach a random identifier generated on your device that is not derived from any device or personal identifier and does not identify you. We record product events such as starting and completing a game, completing your daily mix, continuing a streak, earning a badge, viewing the upgrade screen, and completing a purchase, along with coarse, non-identifying device and diagnostic information. We never send any text you enter or any personal identifiers. See PostHog's Privacy Policy.

The anonymous profile may include a few non-identifying properties, such as your subscription status (free or premium, and which plan) and whether you have enabled notifications. We also use PostHog for remote configuration (feature flags) to decide which features or promotions to show in the app — this is evaluated against the same anonymous identifier and does not collect any additional data about you.

Purchases

If you buy a subscription or the lifetime upgrade, the payment is processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play, and entitlements are managed through our purchases provider, RevenueCat, Inc. To enable and restore purchases, RevenueCat processes:

  • an anonymous app-user identifier generated on your device;
  • purchase and transaction details and your subscription status;
  • basic device and platform information needed to validate the purchase.

This information is used solely to deliver and restore your purchases and premium access. It is never used for advertising. RevenueCat acts as our data processor — see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy. Your actual payment details (card, billing information) are handled entirely by Apple or Google; we never see or store them.

Notifications

With your permission, the app can show an optional local daily reminder to play your daily mix. These notifications are scheduled on your device only. No notification server is used and no data is sent to us. You can turn them off in the app's Settings or in your device settings.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13.

Your rights

Because your games, progress, and settings are stored only on your device, you can erase them at any time by deleting the app. For purchase-related data held by RevenueCat, you may request access or deletion by contacting us. Depending on where you live (for example, the EEA/UK under the GDPR, or California under the CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Our analytics use only anonymous data and are not tied to your identity.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above will reflect any changes.
